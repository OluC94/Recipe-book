Spring Rolls

Ingredients

  • Spring roll wrappers
  • Shredded cabbage
  • Grated carrots
  • Bean sprouts
  • Thinly sliced bell peppers
  • Minced garlic
  • Minced ginger
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Vegetable oil (for frying)
  • Cooked and shredded chicken, shrimp, or tofu (optional)
  • Sliced green onions
  • Chopped cilantro
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the filling by saut√©ing shredded cabbage, grated carrots, bean sprouts, thinly sliced bell peppers, minced garlic, and minced ginger in a pan with a little vegetable oil until softened. Season with soy sauce, sesame oil, salt, and pepper to taste. Optionally, add cooked and shredded chicken, shrimp, or tofu.
  2. Place a spring roll wrapper on a flat surface and spoon some of the filling onto the bottom third of the wrapper.
  3. Roll the wrapper over the filling once, then fold in the sides, and continue rolling tightly until the spring roll is sealed.
  4. Heat vegetable oil in a frying pan or deep fryer over medium heat. Fry the spring rolls in batches until golden brown and crispy, about 2-3 minutes per side.
  5. Remove the spring rolls from the oil and drain on paper towels.
  6. Serve hot with dipping sauce of your choice, garnished with sliced green onions and chopped cilantro.

More details

  • Serves: 1 to 4
  • Course: Starter
  • Suitable for vegetarians
