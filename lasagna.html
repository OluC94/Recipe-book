Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 600g lasagna sheets (6 lasagna sheets)
  • 500g lean beef mince
  • 1 505g jar of Dolmio Tomato Lasagna Sauce
  • 1 490g jar of Dolmio Bechamel Lasagna Sauce
  • 75g grated cheese

Method:

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C (fan 180°C). Heat a large frying pan and add the mince, cook over a high heat, stirring well until browned and cooked through.
  2. Tip in the DOLMIO® Thick Tomato Lasagne Sauce, stir and simmer for 5 minutes, then remove from the heat.
  3. Tip half the mixture into a large rectangular baking dish measuring about 26 cm x 20 cm. Arrange 3 lasagne sheets on top, then spread half the DOLMIO® Bechamel Lasagne Sauce over them. Repeat the layers, then sprinkle the grated cheese evenly over the surface.
  4. Bake for around 30 minutes, until cooked and golden brown. Allow to stand for a few minutes, then serve.
